Screen Definition:

Task details view

Pickup location display

Confirm pickup button

Status indicator

Upload image section

User Story:

As a Volunteer, I want to manage pickup and delivery of assigned food step by step so that I can ensure accurate and traceable food distribution from donor to recipient.

Acceptance Criteria-

• The volunteer should be able to view all assigned delivery tasks.
• The volunteer should confirm pickup only for tasks in “Assigned” status.
• The task status should be updated to “Picked Up” after pickup confirmation and  “Delivered” after completion.
• The duplicate pickup confirmations should be prevented and delivery completion should be allowed only after pickup is confirmed.
• The pickup and delivery timestamp must be recorded.
• It should prevent duplicate delivery confirmations and enforce correct workflow order
• The manager should be notified after pickup and delivery.
• The task should be locked after delivery completion to ensure no further edits.
• The success messages should be displayed after each action.
