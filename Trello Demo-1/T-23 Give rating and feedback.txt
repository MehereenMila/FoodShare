Screen Definition:

Rating Stars

Comment Box (Text area)

Food Quality tags

Submit Feedback button

Average Rating display on profile

User Story:

As a volunteer, I want to rate the quality of food and the donor's punctuality so that the system maintains high safety standards.

Acceptance Criteria-

• The volunteer should rate the donor immediately after clicking "Complete Delivery."
• The system should provide a 1–5 star rating scale and a mandatory "Food Quality" tag selection.
• A user should not submit feedback more than once for the same transaction.
• The system should calculate and display the aggregate "Reliability Score" on the donor's public profile.
