User Stories:

As any user, I want the platform to work fast and smoothly even when thousands of people are using it at the same time for learning and teaching.

Acceptance Criteria:

• All pages should load in under 3 seconds with 100,000+ active users.
• If the page take longer than 3 sec a loading indicator should appear.
• Login and authentication work should be done instantly during peak hours.
• No system crashes or slowdowns should accure during high traffic periods.
